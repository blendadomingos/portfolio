History


The company "Cuido Eu" was founded by Hilena Tocantins in 2019 with the intention of helping her Instagram followers take care of their hair.
Misturinha Pro is the most famous and popular main creation of the businesswoman that restores damaged hair, in addition to leaving it soft, hydrated for longer. On this site, you can purchase Mistura pro, the most varied hair care kits, several varieties of hairbrushes, in addition to other partner brands indicated by the influencer. On the site I take care of, you also have access to tips on how to take care of your hair, videos teaching how to do different hairstyles.
Finally, on the website it is possible for the customer to register the email to purchase offers, in addition to being able to register suggestions and complaints with the idea that the company is always adapting to the needs of its customer.
